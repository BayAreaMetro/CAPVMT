Welcome

Welcome to the RTCI Website and Data Portal. This page is hosted by the Metropolitan Transportation Commission for the transit operators located in the San Francisco Bay Area Region. Access to this website is restricted to MTC staff and users authorized by the primary RTCI contact at each partner agency.

If you already have login credentials, please click on "Login" above. If you need to request permission to access the website, please contact MTC at tromell@mtc.ca.gov to initiate the process. You will need permission from the person designated as the primary RTCI contact at your agency to gain access to the website.

News

2016-04-29

New Website

MTC has launched the second version of the RTCI website. The website now contains enhanced data export capabilities, including data products designed to support use of FTA's TERM Lite model. Please see the new pages listed under the "RTCI Data Portal" navigation menu at the top of the page.

New Website Sections

  • RTCI Database Exports - One click exports of RTCI data tables and forms for running specialized MTC data reports.
  • RTCI Import Tool - Currently disabled: Upload tool for new or updated RTCI data.
  • TERM Lite Compatible Exports - One click exports of RTCI data formatted for TERM Lite compatibility.
  • TERM Lite External Resources - List of externally hosted learning, guidance, and download resources for TERM Lite.

The RTCI 2015 data collection cycle has been finalized. At this time, the import functionality of the website has been disabled. If you need to update your RTCI in the interim, please contact MTC and they will discuss options for updating your dataset. The data submitted for the RTCI 2015 collection is now considered final.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact tromell@mtc.ca.gov
